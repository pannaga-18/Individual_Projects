Tic Tac Toe

GAME RULES

  • Players: Choose ("X" for one player and "O" for the other)
  • Starting Player: The game usually starts with "X", and then with alternate turns.
  • Game Play: Players take turns placing their mark in an empty cell of the grid. Once marked, cannot change.
  • Legal Moves: Players can only place their mark in an empty cell that has not been already marked.
  • Scenarios:
    • Winning: A player wins if they have three of their marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).
    • Draw: If the entire grid is filled without either player achieving a winning line, the game is a draw.
  • Play Again Selection: As 'X' is taken as default in the initial Game. During the re-game, display of the symbol will be effected only after selecting the slot.
